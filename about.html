COMPANY

VISION

Zian Recruitment and Consultancy Agency Limited aims at elimination of poverty by reducing unemployment rates through individual and corporate responsibilities and utilizing the locally and internationally available resources/opportunities. The company looks forward to becoming the river between the job seekers and the employers in Kenya and beyond

COMPANY

MISSION

The company promotes sustainable environmentally sound and cost effective practices in managing job allocations as a resource and connecting job seekers with employers, innovating technology, customer services consultancy and education. Zian Recruitment and Consultancy Agency Limited aims at improving employability rates globally and locally for the economic benefits of the community. The company can also generate tax revenue while fully operating.
